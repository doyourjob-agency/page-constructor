## Parameters

`type: 'benefits-block'`

`titleOne?: string` — Title one.

`postTitleOne?: string` — Selected text in end title one.

`textOne?: string` — Text under title one.

`titleTwo?: string` — Title two.

`postTitleTwo?: string` — Selected text in end title two.

`textTwo?: string` — Text under title two.

`background?: string` — Background block.

`theme?: 'light' | 'dark'` — Theme of text in block.

`accentColor?: string` — Accent color in block.

`cards?: BenefitsBlockCard[]` — Cards.

- `icon?: string` — Card icon link.
- `title?: string` — Card title.
- `text?: string` — Card text.

`items?: BenefitsBlockItem[]` — Items.

- `title: string` — Title of item.
- `title: string` — Text of item.
- `images: string[]` — links to images of item.
- `columns: number[]` — Number of columns of each image.
- `rows: number[]` — Number of rows of each image.

</StoryTemplate>
